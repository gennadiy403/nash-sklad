## REQ-utp-infinite-analytics-retention
**Source:** /Users/genlorem/Projects/oborot-crm/ROADMAP.md
**Description:** Analytics history is retained indefinitely. Marketplace APIs have strict time limits on historical data, and integrators (TopSeller, MPStats, Sellmonitor) do not persist anything locally. OborotCRM stores everything from day one of connection. This is a deliberate architectural choice and must be one of the lead USPs in landing-page and outbound messaging.
**Acceptance criteria:**
- All MP data is persisted in OborotCRM storage from first integration
- Storage growth is accepted as a trade-off
- Landing page surfaces "бесконечное хранение аналитики" as a primary USP
- Outbound / content channels repeat this claim consistently
**Scope:** product / USP / storage architecture
